Where I want to go in Chicago

John Hancock Tower

The skyscraper located in Chicago, USA Along with the Willis Tower, it is one of Chicago’s two skyscrapers, which has 100 floors above ground and 344 meters in height. From 1969 to 2018, when the building opened, it used the John Hancock Center as its name. The name 875 North Michigan Avenue comes from the address of the building and is used temporarily until a new naming convention is signed.

Millenium Park

Millennium Park is a public park located in the Loop community area of Chicago in Illinois operated by the Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs and managed by MB Real Estate.

Cloud gate

Cloud Gate is a public sculpture by Indian-born British artist Sir Anish Kapoor, that is the centerpiece of AT&T Plaza at Millennium Park in the Loop community area of Chicago, Illinois. The sculpture and AT&T Plaza are located on top of Park Grill, between the Chase Promenade and McCormick Tribune Plaza & Ice Rink art institute of chicago
